Our Mission

Rivlosys was founded to provide Canadians with unbiased, bank-grade mortgage calculations without the need for logins or data collection. Our algorithms are updated monthly to reflect OSFI B-20 stress test regulations and provincial land transfer tax changes.

Meet the Team

Our team consists of Canadian software engineers and financial analysts dedicated to housing affordability transparency.
