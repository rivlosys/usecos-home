📰 CMHC Changes 2026: What Homebuyers Need to Know

The new $1.5 million cap on insured mortgages is changing the game for Canadian buyers.

For 2026, the biggest shift in Canadian real estate is the increase of the insured mortgage cap to $1.5 Million. Previously, any home over $1 million required a 20% down payment.

What's Changing?

  • New Price Cap: You can now buy a home up to $1.5M with less than 20% down.
  • 30-Year Amortization: First-time buyers and buyers of new builds can now qualify for 30-year amortizations on insured loans.
  • Stress Test Stability: The qualifying rate remains at the higher of contract rate + 2% or 5.25%.
