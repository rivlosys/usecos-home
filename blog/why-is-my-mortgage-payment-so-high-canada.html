😟 Why is my mortgage payment so high?

Breaking down the factors making Canadian homeownership more expensive in 2026.

The "Payment Shock" Reality

Many Canadians renewing their mortgages in 2026 are seeing payments jump by 30-50%. This is primarily due to the transition from the ultra-low rates of 2021 to the normalized "higher for longer" environment.

3 Main Reasons for High Payments:

  • The Rate Gap: Moving from a 1.9% variable to a 4.5% fixed adds hundreds to your interest costs.
  • Rising Property Taxes: Municipalities across Canada have implemented record-high tax increases to cover budget shortfalls.
  • Negative Amortization: If you had a static-payment variable loan, your balance may have actually increased over the last 3 years.
