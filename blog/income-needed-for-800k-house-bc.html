🏠 How Much Income for an $800k House in BC?

Qualifying for a home in British Columbia requires balancing high prices with the federal stress test rules.

BC Qualification Guide

To buy an $800,000 home in BC with 10% down, a household usually needs an annual income of approximately $170,000 to $190,000, assuming minimal other debts.
