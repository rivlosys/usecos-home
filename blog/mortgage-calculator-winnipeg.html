Qualifying for a Home in Winnipeg

Lenders evaluate your application based on the B-20 Stress Test. Even if your contract rate is 4.5%, you must prove you can handle payments at roughly 6.5%. For an average priced home of $395,000, household income requirements are often upwards of $160,000.
