Buying Property in Ontario?

Ontario has a tiered Land Transfer Tax and offers a rebate of up to $4,000 for first-time homebuyers. Our calculator pre-fills these provincial rules for you.

Ontario Specific Costs

Outside of Toronto, you only pay the Provincial LTT. However, property taxes vary wildly between municipalities like Ottawa, London, and Windsor.
