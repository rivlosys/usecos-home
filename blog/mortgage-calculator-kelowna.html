Qualifying for a Home in Kelowna

Lenders evaluate your application based on the B-20 Stress Test. Even if your contract rate is 4.5%, you must prove you can handle payments at roughly 6.5%. For an average priced home of $780,000, household income requirements are often upwards of $160,000.

Local Market Insights

Purchasing in Kelowna requires careful consideration of British Columbia Property Transfer Tax (PTT) rates. Our calculator factors these into your 'Cash Required' summary so there are no surprises on closing day.
