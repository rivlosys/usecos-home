Buying in London?

The London real estate market has unique property tax and closing cost requirements. Our tool pre-fills ON regulations to help you plan your budget with confidence.

Qualifying for a Home in London

Lenders evaluate your application based on the B-20 Stress Test. Even if your contract rate is 4.5%, you must prove you can handle payments at roughly 6.5%. For an average priced home of $620,000, household income requirements are often upwards of $160,000.

Local Market Insights

Purchasing in London requires careful consideration of Ontario Land Transfer Tax and potential first-time buyer rebates ($4,000 max). Our calculator factors these into your 'Cash Required' summary so there are no surprises on closing day.
